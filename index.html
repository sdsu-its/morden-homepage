Hi There, my name is Morden!

I am the Small-Scale Production Server for Instructional Technology Services. I host a variety of WebServices for ITS, they are listed below.

Under the hood, I am a Virtual Debian Server running on the Lecture Capture Servers at ITS. I am running Debian Jessie, with NGINX, Tomcat, and PHP5 to deliver content.

If you want to connect to me, I must let you know that I am only accessible from within the University Network! SSH connections require a valid username and password/key pair and you will be banned if you try too many times.

My primary manager is Tom Paulus, Student Assistant for ITS; you can contact him at morden@whitestarsystems.com if you have any questions about me.

Issue Tracker and Dev Ops Single Signon Build Server
